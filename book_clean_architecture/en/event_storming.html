Event Storming

How to gather in one day the knowledge that was collected over months.

Event Storming is a workshop format invented by Alberto Brandolini. Business experts and developers gather around a huge wall with sticky notes.

The main rule: we talk about what happened (Events), not about tables and classes.

Sticky Note Legend

Domain Event

Orange
Event (Past Tense)
"Invoice Paid"

Command

Blue
Action (Intent)
"Pay Invoice"

Policy

Purple
Rule (Whenever...)
"If overdue > 3 days"

Aggregate

Yellow
Entity
"Order"

Why is this needed?

  • To find Bounded Contexts: where the language changes or the flow of events breaks.
  • To find Aggregates: yellow stickers appear naturally when we group commands.
  • To see the "blind spots" of the business that no one knew about.
