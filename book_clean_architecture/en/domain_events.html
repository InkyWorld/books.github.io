Domain Events

Side effects and decoupling in clean architecture.

A domain event is a record of something important that happened in the business domain. It is the main tool for building Event-Driven systems and decoupling logic.

Why?

Instead of the Order.pay() method calling EmailService.send(), it simply generates an OrderPaid event. The event handler will send the email. The domain remains clean and independent of infrastructure.

# 1. Event
@dataclass(frozen=True)
class CustomerRegistered:
    customer_id: UUID
    email: str
    registered_at: datetime

# 2. Publishing (usually in UoW after commit)
message_bus.publish(events)
